Curro

Curro is a 3D exploration platformer created in Unreal Engine 4.8.3 over a period of 16 weeks.
With Curro we really wanted to capture that nostalgic experience that we (the developers)
have experienced in our childhoods; with games such as Spyro and/or Jak and Daxter.
[All gameplay intended for children of the age of 14 to 16 years of age]

Columns

Columns is a 'match 3' game that allowed me to explore different programming languages.
Those including JavaScript (Cocos2D Framework), JQuery, HTML and CSS. This project
lasted for about 5 weeks and is also compatible on mobile devices. Columns also stores
highscores locally and has a built in live weather API.

Gravitas

The concept of Gravitas is simple. Alter the direction of gravity to explore the worlds in search for the destination, only hoping to avoid the turrets. It's written in C# Monogame with the physics engine 'Farseer Physics'. Gravitas is compatible on both PC and PS Vita [Development build] thanks to PlayStation Mobile.
